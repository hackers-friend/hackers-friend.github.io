Frequently asked questions about workshops

What will i learn in this workshop ?

We publish agenda of whole workshop on event page. You can check there about each and every topic which will be covered in the workshop

Is there any pre-requisite for this workshop ?

For every workshop we write down whatever we expect you to know, in prerequisite section. You can see there.

Is it mandatory to make payment before registration ?

To generate you entry pass and book your seat we need your transaction ID. Its mandatory that you make payment before registration.

How can be confirmed about my registration ?

You'll receive a confirmation email by us along with your entry passcode within 24 hours. You can also call us for any question. Phone number is given on our event page.
