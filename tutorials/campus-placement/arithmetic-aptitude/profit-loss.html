Profit loss

Campus Placement Training - Prepare step by step for qualifying campus placement

  • Learn
  • Practice problems

Cost price = C.P

Selling price = S.P

Important  Formula :

  • Profit or Gain = S.P - C.P
  • Profit % = (Profit / C.P) * 100 
  • Loss = C.P - S.P
  • Loss % = (Loss / C.P) * 100
  • Profit and Loss are always counted on C.P
  • C.P is always 100% in the case of profit and loss.

Selling Price :

S.P = (100 + Gain % / 100) × C.P

S.P = (100 - Loss % / 100) × C.P

Cost Price :

C.P = [100 / (100 + Gain%) × S.P

C.P = [100 / (100 - Loss%) × S.P
