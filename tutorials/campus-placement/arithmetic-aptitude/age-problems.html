Important Formula :

  • When current age is x,then n times the age is nx.
  • When the current age is x, then age n years later/ hence = x + n.
  • When the current age is x, then age n years ago = x - n
  • The ages in a ratio a : b will be ax and bx.
  • When the current age is x, then 1 / x of the age is x / n.
