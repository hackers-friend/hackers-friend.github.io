Pythagors Theorem:

  • In right angle triangle :-

            (Hypotenuse)² = (Base)² + (Height)²

Important Formula :

Area of square = (side)² = 1 /2 (digonal)²

perimeter of square = 4(side).

  • Area of rectangle = (Length × Breadth)
  • Length of rectangle = Area / Breadth
  • Breadth of rectangle = Area / Length
  • Perimeter of rectangle = 2( Length  + Breadth)
  • Area of a triangle = (1 / 2 ) × Base × Height
  • Area of a triangle = √[s(s-a)(s-b)(s-c)]

          where a, b, c are side the of triangle  and s = 1 / 2( a + b + c)

  • Area of equilateral triangle = (√3 / 4) × (side)²
  • Radius of incircle of an equilateral triangle of side a = a / 2√3
  • Radius of circumcircle of an equilateral triangle of side a = a /√3
  • Area of Parallelogram = (Base × Height)
  • Perimeter of Parallelogram = 2(a + b)
  • Area of rhombus = (1 / 2 )×( d1 ×d2)

          where d1 and d2 are diagonal 

  • Perimeter of rhombus = 2(a + b)
  • Area of Trapezium = (1 / 2) × (sum of parallel sides) × distance between them.
  • Perimeter of Trapezium = AB + BC + CD + AD
  • Area of circle = πR²,where R is the radius.
  • Circumference of a circle = 2πR.
  • Area of Semi-circle = πR² / 2
  • Circumference of a semi-circle = πR.
