Relation in Time, Distance and Speed

  • Speed = Distance / Time
  • Time = Distance / Speed
  • Distance = Speed × Time

Converting Km/hr to m/sec

  • A km/hr = A × (5/18) m/sec

Converting m/sec to Km/hr

  • A m/sec = A × (18/5) Km/hr

Other important Points

  • the times taken by then to cover the same distance isIf the ratio of speeds of A and B is a:b then, the ratio of time taken by them to cover the same distance is 1/a : 1/b or b:a
  • If a man covers a certain distance at the speed of x Km/hr and then an equal distance at y Km/hr then the average speed during whole journey would be 2xy/(x+y)
