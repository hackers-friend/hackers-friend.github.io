1. Three taps A,B and C can fill a tank in 12, 15 and 20 hours respectively. If A is open all the time and B and C are open for 1 hours each alternately, the tank will be full in :

A. 6 hrs

B. 20/3 hrs

C. 7 hrs

D. 15/2 hrs

Answer: Option C

D. 90
