Simple Interest :

S.I = (P × R × T )/ 100

where P → Principal

         R → Rate (R% per annum (p.a.))

         T → Time (T years)

When S.I is added to principal, It is change in to amount.

P + S.I = A

S.I = A - P   , (A  → Amount)

Case 1: If S.I , R & T are known,

P = (S.I × 100 ) / (R × T)

Case 2 : If S.I, P & T ,are known,

R = (S.I × 100 ) / ( P × T)

Case 3 : If S.I, P & R are known,

T = (S.I × 100 ) / (P × R)
