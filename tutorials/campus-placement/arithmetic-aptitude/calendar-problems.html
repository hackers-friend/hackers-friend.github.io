Odd Days :

We are supposed to find the day of the week  on the given date.

For this, we use the concept  of 'odd days'.

In the given period, the number of days more than the complete weeks are called odd days.

Leap Year :

Every year divisible by 4 is a leap year, if it is not a century year.

Every 4th century is a leap year and no other century is a leap year.

Odinary Year :

The year which is not a leap year is called an ordinary years. An ordinary year has 365 days.

Counting Of Odd Days :

1 ordinary year = 365 days = (52 weeks + 1 days).

1 ordinary year has 1 odd day.

1 leap year = 366 days = (52 weeks + 2 days).

1 leap year has 2 odd days.
