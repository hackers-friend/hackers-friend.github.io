15. Two numbers A and B are such that the sum of 5% of A and 4% of B is two-third of the sum of 6% of A and 8% of B. Find the ratio of A : B.

A. 1 : 1

B. 1 : 2

C. 4 : 3

D. 2 : 1

Answer: Option C

Explanation: 5 % of A + 4 % of B = 2/3(6% of A + 8 % of B) =>5A / 100 + 4B / 100 = 2/3(6A/100 + 8B/100) => 5A +4B = 2/3(6A +8B) => 15A + 12B = 12A +16B =>3A = 4B =>A / B = 4 / 3 => A : B = 4 : 3
