How to write effective resume

Your resume is a medium for advertising yourself and your qualifications, skills, and ambitions. It is a record of your goals and successes, and a way of communicating your capabilities to a potential employer.

Point to remember:

·         Don’t over-complicate things. Unless you are in the creative industry, all a resume truly needs is the four sections:

o   Summary of Qualifications

o   Education and Certifications

o   Work Experience

o   Technical Skills [or Additional Skills and Activities].

·         State your most important points first. Be sure to mention your degree in your summary of qualifications to make sure hiring managers don’t overlook it.

·         Make it readable and printable.

·         Times New Roman or Arial font, no smaller than 11 point.

·         Set your margins no less than .5 inches all around and don’t change the page setup from Letter size paper. 

You have to prepare your resume in such a way that it gives impression in first look that you are a perfect fit for the job you have applied.

I will explain to you the step by step process of creating effective resume:

Step 1:  Address Section:-

This section will be on top of your resume giving a detail about your name, address, phone number and e-mail address. For example:-

Full Name

City, State

Pin Code

   # Mobile Number

   Email Id

 

Step 2:  Carrier Objective:-

A resume objective also referred to as a career objective, is a 2-3 sentence statement that provides an overview of your skills.

For example:-

·         To work with an organization, where I can build algorithm and write programs, which make people lives better.

 

·         Result oriented, self driven, highly motivated, smart and hungry to learn new technologies, methodologies, strategies and processes.

  

Step 3: Educational Detail:-

Now you have to start telling about our educational background. There are many organizations who give lot of preference to your educational background. So it is important to tell about your academic records. You should put your highest degree on top and then lower qualifications at lower levels.

For Example:-

 

Ø  2013-17, A B.E (Computer Science & Engineering) with 8.12 CGPA (out of 10.0) From Indian Institute of Technology, Patna.

Ø  2011-13, Completed 12th (Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry) with 83.6% from CBSE.

Ø  2011, Completed 10th with 77.8 % from CBSE.

 

Step 4: Technical Skills:-

Next section in your resume will present your Technical Skills. This section will start with telling your major skills on which you have good command. So list down all the skills first where you are fully confident and then keep rest of the skills at lower levels.

For Example:-

Programming Languages: C, C++, JAVA

Operating Systems: Red Hat Linux 9.0, Windows XP

Databases: SQL, MySQL, Sybase

Protocols: SMTP, FTP, POP3, IMAP

Tools & Technologies: J2EE, JSP, CORBA

Others: Socket Programming, Multithreading, SDLC, OOP, UML

Step 5: Project Details:-

Now comes real story and this is really important to show your all the projects, assignments etc. Reader should understand what exactly you have done in past. Here you will put hands on experience with different type of projects, people and tools and technologies. You have to be clear on what was your organizations, year of the project, roles, responsibilities, achievements etc. On each and every project or position. You should also mention about what were the tools and technologies you have used during that project.

For Example:-

Project Name: project name

Project Description: It is an intelligent question answer program (CLI). It reads txt files written in English, process them, and find their meanings using NLTK and replies to questions related to that.

Duration: 1 month

Team Size: 4

Role Played: Database Designer

Skills Used: MySql, Python

 

 

Step 6: Achievements and Extra Curricular:-

For Example:-

Ø  Participated in Best Out of Waste Conducted by ISTE Chapter in IIT.

Ø  Executive Member of Event Organizing Committee in Indian Institute of Technology.

Ø  In class 10th I’m the topper of our school in 2011.

Ø  Actively participated in various sports activities for inter school.

 

 

Step 7: Interests/Hobbies:-

 For Example:-

·         Playing Cricket

·         Watching Movies

·         Learn new Technology

·         Story Writing
