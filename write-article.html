What not to do while writing article

→ Don't copy

Writing articles is all about telling your story. Keep it that way. Don't copy. Be yourself.

→ Don't go too long

Too much of anything becomes boring. Be small enought to maintain interest and long enough to cover up the subject.

→ Don't be outdated

Whats is new is what people love to read about. Know whats new and write about it.
