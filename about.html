About HackersFriend

What do we do ?

We host shows about technology, Algorithm designing, Natural Language Processing, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science and a lot of stuffs.

Our goal is to spread the innovation and share technologies, which can make people's lives better.

We believe in building things. Nowadays, it has been seen that, people do focus on certificates than practical knowledge. We want to end this culture. So, We do certify user's based upon their ability to build things. If you see someone bearing our certificate, it means they have built something in front of us. We are building the culture of creativity driven corporate society.

What is our mission ?

Our mission is to spread the technology, inspire developers to build things to make people's lives better. In every show, we try to build something new and cool. Goal is to inspire developers around the world to build impossible but necessary things for goodness of society.

Who are we ?

We are a group of geek, tech enthusiast guys, trying to develop a culture of creativity in corporate world.
